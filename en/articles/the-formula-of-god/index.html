The Formula of God

God exists. And he does not.

Say it however it comes out, and you will not be mistaken.

Because God (All That Exists) is information possessing a binary code: “yes” and “no” at the same time.

Because eternity belongs only to analogues-antipodes whose parameters, taken together, in pairs, in any phase of formation and development, are described mathematically, presenting in sum a zero dimension; and zero is a complex number whose expanded notation implies the presence of an imaginary, “virtual” part, and in this form corresponds both to the concept of “everything” and to the concept of “nothing” at the same time.

Here it is, the Formula of God:

x + x(i²) = nothing = something

Complicated? And who said it had to be easy?

(“x” is a certain number expressing the sum of all baryonic mass of the universe)
