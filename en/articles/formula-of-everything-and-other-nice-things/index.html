The Formula of Everything and Other Nice Things

Recognizing the existence of negative time makes it possible: to discard the need for any external, supernatural force (a Creator) for the emergence of the Universe.

to propose a reliable FORMULA OF EVERYTHING:

E - E = E + i^2E (i squared multiplied by E)

E = mc^2, if anyone remembers;

to explain:

  • the nature of the Big Bang,
  • quantum-wave dualism,
  • the cause of the curvature of space,
  • the operating principle of the Universe - a unique Perpetual Motion Machine,
  • the meaning of the category of time,
  • the Twin Paradox,
  • the preferability of Friedmann’s First Model,
  • the mathematical Formula of Everything,
  • the inevitability of superluminal velocities,
  • the existence of a CPT-symmetric world,
  • the tachyonic mass of antiparticles,
  • the violation of cause-and-effect relationships in the “looking-glass world,”
  • the complementarity of entropy and information,
    -the eternity of these physical concepts,
  • the existence of a certain single fundamental essence undergoing a ‘phase transition’ depending on velocity,
  • the nature of gravity (and electricity),
  • the composition of dark matter and energy,
    and all that sort of thing, including...“the difficult character of the Lord God Almighty.”
